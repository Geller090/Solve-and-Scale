ABOUT US

We're not another big agency. We're makers helping makers since 2020.

Based in Point Loma, CA, Slab & System was founded by Taylor Geller in 2020 as a boutique digital partner for furniture sellers and handmade brands. We work closely with our clients—like we're in the shop with you—helping modernize websites, organize inventory, and get seen online through practical guidance that actually makes sense.

No complex funnels or enterprise solutions. Just honest, grounded help with your Shopify site, Google listings, email setup, and simple tracking dashboards so you can see what's working without logging into 10 different tools.
